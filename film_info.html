While aspects of film, animation, and video editng have always interested me, I began my formal training in the prestigious Ballard High School Digital Film Making Program. It was here I wrote, produced, lit, edited, puppeteered, and cameod in several awarded shorts. Since then, my work has been relegated to smaller video-editing projects - editing along side writing remains my greatest skill in the field.

SARP UW Rocket Unveiling

2019

As part of the Society for Advanced Rocket Propulsion business team, I was tasked to make a video showcasing a years worth of work at the club's largest event: unveiling. After pouring through hours of footage (some of it taken myself), texturing, lighting, and rendering our CAD model, and co-writing an epic classic rock song, I put together this video for its premiere infront of roughly 300 people.

My Bike and Me

2016

A 3-person-team documentary that follows the perilous journey of Duncan Star Boszko as he learns how to ride a bike. Production, editing, and music direction was done by yours truly.
